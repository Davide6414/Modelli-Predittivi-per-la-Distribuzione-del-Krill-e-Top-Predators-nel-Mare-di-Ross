Overview of Data

The study relies on historical data collected during the 1994-1995 Antarctic research campaigns in the Ross Sea. These datasets include information on krill distribution, environmental descriptors, and predator activity.

Datasets

  • Krill Data: Acoustic surveys capturing krill density and distribution.
  • Environmental Data: Temperature, salinity, and ice coverage data layers extracted from remote sensing and in-situ measurements.
  • Predator Observations: Visual census of seabirds and marine mammals, including spatially referenced records.

Tools and Software

QGIS and R programming were utilized for data processing, geospatial analysis, and model implementation. Random Forest algorithms were applied to generate predictive distributions.
